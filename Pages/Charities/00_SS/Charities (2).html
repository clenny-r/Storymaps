Spotlight

A special report on the December 2025 Street Feeding in Spanish Town — a day that showed the true heart of TWC Charity.

A Mission of Compassion

TWC Charity's street feeding in Spanish Town was a powerful expression of love and service to the homeless community. Volunteers came together with one purpose: to help those in need. The atmosphere was filled with warmth as meals were shared, conversations were had, and eager faces lit up with gratitude.

Gratitude for Dedicated Servants

This meaningful outreach would not have been possible without the strong leadership of the TWC Charity leader and the unwavering support of the committee members. Heartfelt thanks go out to every volunteer, donor, and supporter who contributed their time, resources, and effort. Each person played an important role, and their selfless service reflected true unity and compassion in action.

Giving God the Glory

Above all, we give God all the glory for making this event a success. We believe this is God's work, and we are honored to be His hands and feet in the community. The experience has left us eager and excited to do it again, continuing to serve with love, faith, and dedication as we answer the call to help those in need.

Annual Initiative

Back-to-School
Drive

Every year, TWC Charities equips children from low-income families with everything they need to start school with confidence. No child should miss out on education because of what they lack.

School Supplies

Notebooks, pens, pencils, rulers, and all the tools children need to learn and thrive in the classroom.

School Bags

Durable backpacks so every child can carry their books and supplies with pride on their first day of school.

Uniforms

School uniforms provided so children can attend school looking their best, removing the stigma of poverty from the classroom.
