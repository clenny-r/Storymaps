Our Ministries

Every member. Every season. Every calling.
Serving God together through purposeful, Spirit-led community.

Kingdom Work in Action

Built to Serve,
Empowered to Transform

At TWC, ministry is not the work of a few — it is the calling of every believer. Each ministry within our church is a vessel through which God moves: nurturing faith, strengthening families, discipling the next generation, and covering our community in prayer. Whether you are finding your place or deepening your service, there is a ministry for you.

"From him the whole body, joined and held together by every supporting ligament, grows and builds itself up in love, as each part does its work."

Ephesians 4:16
Our Ministries

Where Every Voice Belongs

Six ministries. One body. United by the Spirit of God and anchored in His Word.

Women's Ministry

Women of Purpose

The Women's Ministry of TWC is a place of grace, strength, and spiritual growth. We gather women of all ages to be encouraged in their faith, equipped for daily living, and empowered to walk confidently in the purpose God has placed within them. Through fellowship, Bible study, outreach, and seasonal events, we celebrate womanhood as a gift and calling from God.

"She is clothed with strength and dignity; she can laugh at the days to come."

Proverbs 31:25
Men's Ministry

Men of Courage

The Men's Ministry calls men to rise as pillars of faith — in their homes, their church, and their communities. We cultivate godly character, accountability, and servant leadership through Scripture-based fellowship, mentorship, and community service. We believe that when men are transformed by God, families and communities are transformed with them.

"Be on your guard; stand firm in the faith; be courageous; be strong."

1 Corinthians 16:13
Children's Ministry

Kingdom Kids

Kingdom Kids is where young hearts encounter Jesus in a safe, joyful, and age-appropriate environment. We partner with parents to lay a strong biblical foundation in the lives of children, nurturing their faith through worship, Scripture memory, creative activities, and Spirit-filled teaching. Every child at TWC is known, valued, and loved.

"Let the little children come to me… for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these."

Matthew 19:14
Teens Ministry

TWC Ignite

TWC Ignite is a ministry that meets teenagers where they are and walks with them toward who God created them to be. In a culture filled with noise and confusion, we offer clarity, community, and the truth of God's Word. Through relevant teaching, peer discipleship, outreach, and creative expression, our teens are being shaped into the next generation of kingdom builders.

"Don't let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example."

1 Timothy 4:12
Praise & Worship Ministry

Sound of Worship

The Praise and Worship Ministry leads our congregation into the manifest presence of God through anointed music and heartfelt adoration. Our singers, musicians, and media team work together to create an atmosphere where lives are changed before a single word of preaching is spoken. We believe worship is not just a programme segment — it is the heartbeat of everything we do at TWC.

"Worship the Lord with gladness; come before him with joyful songs."

Psalm 100:2
Prayer Ministry

House of Prayer

Prayer is the engine of everything at TWC. The Prayer Ministry intercedes for our congregation, our nation of Jamaica, and the nations of the world. Through our weekly prayer meeting, corporate intercession, and personal prayer chains, we believe that the fervent, effectual prayers of righteous people make a profound difference. You are invited to bring your needs — and your faith.

"My house will be called a house of prayer for all nations."

Isaiah 56:7
Take the Next Step

Find Your Place in Ministry

Every believer has a gift. Every gift has a place. Reach out to us and we will help you discover where you fit in the body of Christ at TWC.
