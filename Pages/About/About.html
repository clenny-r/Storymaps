Transformational Worship Centre

Who We Are

Transforming minds, changing lives.
A kingdom-building community serving Jamaica and the world.

Our Mission

Transform Lives Through
Worship & Empowerment

TWC seeks to transform lives through worship and empowerment, seeking interventions in communities. We believe that genuine encounter with God changes people from the inside out.

Our Vision

A Kingdom Building
Organisation

We are a kingdom-building organisation making lasting Christ-like disciples. Every programme, every service, and every outreach effort flows from this singular vision.

Article 2

Our
Objectives

Rooted in Scripture and led by the Spirit, the objectives of the church guide everything we do — from Sunday worship to community outreach and every ministry in between.

a

Make Disciples Make disciples of Jesus the Christ; spread the word about the Kingdom of God, baptize and teach.

b

Facilitate Worship Facilitate the worship of God in Spirit and in truth, creating spaces for genuine encounter.

c

Community & Communion Promote the communion of the saints and assist the needy within and beyond our congregation.

d

Holistic Development Foster the holistic development of individuals and the community through Spirit-led programmes.

Article 4

Our
Teachings

Grounded in the Word of God. Guided by the Holy Spirit. Lived out in community.

The core teachings of the church are Biblical principles of the Scriptures. Members follow our teachings based on their Spirit-led conscience.

Our doctrinal positions are found in our By-Laws listed under the title "Church Teachings", which we encourage all members and visitors to read as they grow in their faith journey with us.

2 Timothy 3:16
