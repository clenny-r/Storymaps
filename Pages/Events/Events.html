Why the Sabbath Still Matters: Rest, Renewal, and the Creator’s Gift

In a world that glorifies busyness, the Sabbath stands as a radical act of trust. God did not merely suggest rest — He sanctified it. From Genesis to Revelation, the seventh-day Sabbath threads through Scripture as both a memorial of creation and a foretaste of the eternal rest promised to God’s people. This post explores why Sabbath observance is not a burden but one of the greatest gifts available to the believer today.
