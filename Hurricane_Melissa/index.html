Storm Surge Simulation — Jamaica

Hurricane
Melissa

Interactive Inundation Maps · All 14 Parishes

Coastal engineering simulation of storm surge and inundation across Jamaica's 14 parishes. Three model scenarios explore the range of potential flood impacts based on HURRICANE MELISSA FORECAST/ADVISORY NUMBER 18.

Surge Only
Surge + Est. Setup
Surge + Setup + Rain
Surge Only — Models the direct wind-driven storm surge component. Represents the baseline coastal inundation from the storm's wind field and pressure gradient pushing water onshore.
Surge + Estimated Setup — Adds an estimated wind setup component to the surge model, accounting for longer-fetch wave-driven water level rise across coastal embayments.
Surge + Estimated Setup & Rain — The most comprehensive scenario. Includes storm surge, wave setup, and rainfall-driven inland flooding, representing the upper bound of combined flood risk.
